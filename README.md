# GovMotion

A research dashboard tracking 100 technology companies entering or early in the federal / SLED government market.

## Live dashboard

https://knutesteel.github.io/govmotion/

## What's here

`index.html` is a self-contained, single-file dashboard. No build step, no dependencies, no server. Open it in any modern browser.

Companies were surfaced from FedRAMP and GovRAMP announcements, recent funding rounds, YC defense and gov batches, GovTech 100 newcomers, DIU/SBIR awards, and federal/SLED hiring signals.

## Features

- 100 companies with overview, revenue range, headcount, and the entry signal that surfaced them
- - Market filter pills: Federal / S&L / Both
  - - Action flags per company (multi-select): Star / Target / Partner / Not Likely
    - - Search across name, overview, and tags
      - - Sort: default / A-Z / by status
        - - Two views: Cards and Table (last view choice is remembered)
          - - State persists in your browser via localStorage
           
            - ## Notes
           
            - Revenue and employee figures are public-source estimates expressed as ranges - useful for triage, not for diligence.
